At the start of the game the player has no score and no upgrades, they acquire score by defeating enemies using both their rotating weapon that follows their cursor and fired with Mouse 1 or by using the fixed weapon at the front of their ship with the spacebar.
When the player dies, they’ll be prompted to enter the shop by pressing “O”. Where they will be able to take their accumulated score into the shop to configure their ship for their next attempt.
The main shop is comprised of 3 main sections of content. You’ve got the ship preview on the left where you can see the currently equipped hull and weapons. The various upgrade options on the top right. And the selected upgrade details on the bottom right.
Featuring both mobile enemies that seek the player as well as static enemies that target the player once they’re in range.
Further in the level there are boss enemies that attempt to challenge the player as well as begin to close the gap in the players ability to complete the game without any upgrades. However, the design of the game will still make it possible for the player to beat the game without upgrades but, only as a challenge.
As the game progresses more and more enemies are found with better weapons attached to them in order to incrementally raise the skill ceiling as the game continues.

Nova Zone is a take on the arcade style shmup game genre where the player progresses through an environment full of progressively more difficult enemies that they need to destroy. The twist being that they can sacrifice their score to acquire upgrades to make themselves more powerful. Consequently, this gives the player ownership of the difficulty of the game in a way they can immediately see its impact, allowing them to play it their way.
