The Last Dance

Project Type: Game Development

Engine Used: Unity

Languages Used: C#, UXML

Primary Role: Developer

The Last Dance is a multiplayer cooperative versus real time strategy game. Where 2 Champions fight against an invasion of demonic forces lead by an demon Overlord player in an RTS vs COOP showdown. Our Champions liberate villages to gather points to upgrade and unlock new abilities to destroy the Overlord's base. While the Overlord must capture villages to gain stronger to summon in their army in order to crush the Champions..
Developed using Unity Netcode for Gameobjects.
