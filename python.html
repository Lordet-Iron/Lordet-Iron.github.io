The player’s primary method of fighting enemies is the usage of the “Spacebar” to shoot enemies. Each shoot consumes an amount of energy displayed at the bottom left of the screen in blue. This energy recharges overtime but the player is encouraged by this mechanic to only fire when necessary or they’ll have a reduced fire rate. Thus, rewarding a more conservative play style when playing the game.
Enemies drop health packs on death which the player can fly towards to regain health over the course of the game.
Especially on the easy difficulties of the game the player will be accompanied by friendly ships that will aid the player throughout the game.
